# AI Relationship Coach - Visual Demo Guide 🎯

## Quick Start: How to Access the AI Coach

### Step 1: Navigate to Events
1. Open your AURA app
2. Look at the bottom navigation bar
3. Click on the **Calendar** icon (📅) labeled "Events"
4. You'll be taken to the Events hub

### Step 2: Explore the Three Main Tabs

#### 🌟 Tab 1: AI Suggestions
- **What you'll see**: Personalized event recommendations
- **Features**:
  - Events matched to your personality and preferences
  - Match scores showing compatibility
  - AI insights preview on each event card
  - "Great for First Dates" badges
  
#### 👥 Tab 2: Community
- **What you'll see**: User-suggested date spots
- **Features**:
  - Browse community recommendations
  - Upvote great suggestions (thumbs up button)
  - See who suggested each venue
  - Filter by event type
  
#### 💡 Tab 3: Date Tips
- **What you'll see**: AI-powered first date guidance
- **Features**:
  - Select event type (Coffee Shop, Concert, etc.)
  - Get personalized tips including:
    - What to wear
    - Conversation topics
    - Etiquette advice
    - Red flags to watch for

## Feature Walkthroughs

### 🎨 Suggesting a New Date Spot

1. Click the **"Suggest Spot"** button (top right)
2. Fill in the form:
   - Select category (e.g., Coffee Shop ☕)
   - Enter venue name
   - Describe why it's great for dates
   - Add location details
   - Set price range (₦ symbols)
   - Choose atmosphere tags
   - Add helpful tips
3. Click **"Suggest Event"**
4. Your suggestion will appear after approval

### 💬 Using Conversation Starters

1. Go to the Events page
2. Scroll to the AI Relationship Coach section
3. Click on **"Starters"** tab
4. Select a match from your conversations
5. Click **"Generate"**
6. Browse AI-generated openers filtered by tone:
   - 🤔 Curious
   - 😄 Playful
   - 😊 Warm
   - 😏 Flirty
   - 💝 Sincere
7. Click **Copy** to use any opener
8. Paste into your chat!

### 📊 Checking Relationship Health

1. Open AI Relationship Coach dashboard
2. View your health scores:
   - Overall Score (center gauge)
   - Communication (blue)
   - Trust (green)
   - Respect (yellow)
   - Emotional Connection (pink)
3. Read recent insights
4. Track your progress over time

### 🛡️ Learning About Red Flags

1. Go to AI Relationship Coach
2. Click **"Education"** tab
3. Browse categories:
   - Communication issues
   - Boundary violations
   - Controlling behavior
   - Respect indicators
   - Consistency concerns
   - Emotional safety
4. Learn healthy relationship signs

## Sample User Journey

### Scenario: Planning a First Date

**Step 1**: Browse AI Suggestions
```
User clicks Events → AI Suggestions tab
Sees: "Coffee & Conversation" - 95% match
Notice: "Great for First Dates" badge
Action: Clicks RSVP
```

**Step 2**: Get Date Tips
```
User clicks Date Tips tab
Selects: "Coffee Shop"
Reads: 
  - What to wear: "Smart casual"
  - Duration: "1-2 hours"
  - Conversation topics: ["Favorite coffee orders", "Travel stories"]
  - Etiquette: "Offer to pay, respect if they split"
```

**Step 3**: Prepare with Conversation Starters
```
User goes to Chat with their match
Opens AI Relationship Coach
Generates conversation starters
Copies: "I noticed we're both into hiking too! 
         What got you started with that?"
```

**Step 4**: Propose the Date
```
In chat, user sends:
"Hey! I found this cozy café called 'The Coffee Bean' 
and thought it might be fun. Want to check it out together?"
Match accepts!
```

**Step 5**: After the Date
```
User returns to AI Coach
Rates the experience
Gets personalized feedback
Relationship health score updates
```

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: AI Coach not loading
**Solution**: Refresh page, check internet connection

### Issue: No event suggestions showing
**Solution**: Complete your profile with preferred date types

### Issue: Can't upvote events
**Solution**: Ensure you're logged in and verified

### Issue: Conversation starters seem generic
**Solution**: Complete more profile sections for better personalization
