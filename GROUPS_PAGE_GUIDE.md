## Quick Actions Guide ⚡

### Create a Group
1. Go to Groups page
2. Click **"+ Group"** button (top right)
3. Fill in details (name, category, description)
4. Click **"Create Group"**

### Create an Event
**Option A: Standalone Event**
1. Go to Groups page
2. Click **"+ Event"** button (top right)
3. Toggle **"Share with Community"** if desired
4. Fill in event details
5. Click **"Create Event"**

**Option B: Group Event**
1. Go to Groups tab
2. Find your group
3. Click **"View Events"**
4. Click **"Create Event"**
5. Fill in details

### Suggest a Date Spot
1. Go to Groups page
2. Click **"AI Picks"** tab
3. Click **"Suggest Spot"** button
4. Fill in venue details
5. Click **"Suggest Event"**

### Use AI Coach
1. Go to Groups page
2. Scroll to AI Relationship Coach section
3. Browse tabs:
   - **Dashboard**: Health scores
   - **Starters**: Conversation openers
   - **Tips**: Communication advice
   - **Education**: Red flags info

## Common Questions ❓

### Q: Where did my Events go?
**A:** All your events are still there! Just click the **"Group Events"** tab in the Groups page.

### Q: How do I create events now?
**A:** Same as before! Click the **"+ Event"** button at the top of the Groups page.

### Q: Can I still get AI date suggestions?
**A:** Yes! Even better now. Click the **"AI Picks"** tab for personalized recommendations.

### Q: Is the AI Coach still available?
**A:** Yes! It's always visible at the top of the Groups page now.

### Q: What if I only want to see groups?
**A:** Click the **"Groups"** tab to focus only on groups.

### Q: Can I still RSVP to events?
**A:** Absolutely! All event features work exactly the same.
